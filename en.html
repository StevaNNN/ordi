What is ORDI and for whom is it intended?

ORDI is an application designed to restaurants / cafes that want to improve their business and for customers who will finally lose main issues when visiting a catering facility:

  1. "Will we finally be served!?"
  2. "When will the waiter pass in order to pay?"
  3. "Confusing menu, I really don't know who will read this with ease!?"
  4. "How they mean "we don't have it", why that's not pointed somewhere!?"

ORDI handles this in many ways and we will do our best to help you explained them. So let's get started:

  1. On each table of the catering facility there is a pamphlet with clear indications of where and how the customer can download application on Google Play store by simply scanning a QR code. After that the customer chooses whether wants to create an account on the app or simply scan the QR code that can also be found on pamphlet and the role is to open a personalized menu of the catering facility.
  2. The customer has an option on their mobile application  "Call waiter"  which automatically signals to the staff from which table their presence has been requested. Also next to the option  "Call waiter" there are also options  "Pay with card"  or  "Pay with cash"  which signal to the staff the method of payment chosen by the customer from the given table.
  3. The restaurant menu should generally be updated by the staff, if item is missing, the staff has an option "Hide Item" which will automatically delete the same item from the menu which the customer is looking at their mobile application. One item in the menu contains, among other things, a name, a picture, description, size and of course the price, in this way the customer is informed in advance about the content of the desired item or about any allergens it carries.
  4. Our ordering system works in real time so that the moment you choose from the menu what you want to eat / drink, the staff received a notification of what was ordered and from which table. The waiter then checks with the chef and bartender on the balance of the ordered items, then informs the customer via a message as to whether the order has been approved or rejected.
